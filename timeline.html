Chronology

From the Charter to the present

A working chronology of the University of Sussex. Entries are provisional and will be expanded and sourced as the history is written.

  • 1961
    Royal Charter.

    The University of Sussex is established by Royal Charter and admits its first students.

  • 1962
    Coat of arms granted.

    The College of Arms grants the university its arms; Sussex is the first British university granted supporters. See The Arms.

  • 1960s
    The Falmer campus.

    Basil Spence's buildings rise on the Downs at Falmer; Falmer House is later listed Grade I. See Campus.

  • —
    Expansion and the schools of study.

    The arts and sciences organised into interdisciplinary schools rather than departments. (To be written.)
