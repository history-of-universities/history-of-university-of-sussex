An indicative bibliography

A working bibliography for the history of the University of Sussex, organised by theme: the mid-century debate about the idea of the university, the new universities of the 1960s, and the documentary record of the founding of Sussex itself. It draws in the first instance on the reading assembled for the essay that anchors this project.
