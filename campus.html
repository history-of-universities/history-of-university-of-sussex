The campus · Falmer

Spence's university on the Downs

The University of Sussex was built at Falmer, in a fold of the South Downs outside Brighton, to designs by Sir Basil Spence. In concrete, brick, and plate glass, Spence gave the new university a visual identity as deliberate as its curriculum. Falmer House, at its heart, is listed Grade I.
